Getting Started with Our Website


Introduction

Welcome to our website! This help article will guide you through the basics and help you get started quickly. Whether you are new to our platform or need a refresher, we've got you covered.


Creating an Account

To access all the features of our website, you'll need to create an account. Follow these steps to get started:

  •      •  Click on the "Account/login" button located on the top right corner, and from there select "sign up"
  •      •  Fill in the required information, such as your username, email address, and password.
  •      •  Click "Submit" to create your account.

Navigating the Website

Once you've logged in, you'll find a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to navigate through our website. Here are some key elements you should be familiar with:

  •      •  Menu Bar: The menu bar is located at the top of the page and provides quick access to different sections of the website, such as Home, mini-Games, Teams info, and Account Settings.
  •      •  Dashboard: The dashboard is your personalized homepage where you can view relevant information, updates, and notifications.

Exploring Games and Tournaments

Our website offers a wide range of games and tournaments for you to enjoy. Here's how you can explore and participate:

  •      •  Games: Visit the Games section to browse through the available games. Click on a game to view detailed information, such as rules, gameplay, and user reviews.
  •      •  Tournaments: In the Tournaments section, you can find ongoing and upcoming tournaments. Join a tournament by following the registration process and be sure to check the tournament schedule for important dates and times.

Managing Your Account

To make the most of your experience on our website, take advantage of the account management features:

  •      •  Profile: Update your profile information, including your username, e-mail, and bio. This helps other users get to know you better.
  •      •  Balance: Keep an eye on your account balance, which reflects your virtual currency or rewards earned through games and tournaments.
  •      •  Settings: Customize your account settings, such as theme prefeed and password settings, to tailor your experience to your preferences.

Getting Help and Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions while using our website, we're here to help. Here are some resources you can use:

  •      •  Password reset: In case you forget you're password or initial data.
  •      •  Contact Us: Feel free to reach out to our support team via the Contact Us page at anytime and the stuff will help you.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You're now equipped with the essential knowledge to navigate our website and make the most of your experience. Remember, if you ever need assistance, refer back to this help article or reach out to our friendly support team or you can as well download the website report.

We hope you enjoy your time on our website and have a great gaming experience!
